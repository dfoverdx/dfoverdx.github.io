Fail's 12-Hour Stream

Fail plans on streaming for 12 hours next Sunday, October 14th! He would like chat's input on which games to play. Now's your chance to watch him play all those games you suggested, he said he would play, and then never did because he's lazy .

How to submit game suggestions

First check to see that your game has not already been submitted to save your hard-working, dedicated mods (and Ender) some headaches. If your game hasn't been submitted, add the title and platform in the #game-suggestions channel.

If you are submitting a multiplayer game, please include the platform, how many players can play at once, and roughly how long each round lasts.

How games will be chosen

When your mods are bored enough, they'll periodically peruse #game-suggestions and add the titles to #game-votes. Vote by adding your failsRigged reaction to games that you want to see played. The games with the most votes come Sunday are the most likely to be played.

Fail will have ultimate veto power on suggested games. That said, everyone knows FailsGov is a benevolent dictatorship. And let's be honest, when has he ever denied a chat's overwhelming majority?

If you see a multiplayer game that you'd be interested in playing with him, add a failsMini reaction, too.

How players will be selected for multiplayer

We're not sure yet.

If I (dx_over_dt) and/or other developers in chat have time before Sunday, it might be a sophisticated system with some shiny algorithms and a fabulous UI/website.

If not (and this is the more likely case), it will involve some random picks that are totally not rigged in favor of mods. And hey, just because I happen to get picked for every game, there's no way to prove that it didn't happen by random chance.
